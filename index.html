Hello from Anshul Garg!

Welcome to my home on the Web! You found your way here, you might as well stay a while.

I'm focused on solving problems at the intersection of technology, strategy and behavioral science, with a background in startups, technology and design.

As a developer, my main tools are Python, Ruby on Rails and PostgreSQL, in addition to my extensive experience in MongoDB and HTML/CSS/JavaScript. My past experience includes technology strategy and architecting fault-tolerant systems that handled hundreds of thousands of unique users.

As a designer, I'm focused on building smart, elegant and intuitive interfaces with a focus on accomplishing specific user behaviors and developing an enjoyable experience, incorporating concepts in psychology.

As a strategist, I study complex systems and strategies on navigating competitive and adversarial environments. On the business side, I'm focused on creating businesses with coherent customer acquisition, customer retention and business development strategies. I also concern myself with strategies on scaling startup teams and maintaining optimal communication within teams.

I spend most of my time doing stuff, and ignoring grown-up things like health insurance and owning more than one set of sheets. And if you haven't realized yet, I hate writing in third person.
